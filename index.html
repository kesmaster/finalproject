Languages Through Generations: Where the European language landscape is headed today

Over the last three generations, the number of learned languages has grown dramatically and become more diverse across Europe. There is a higher percentage of younger people are learning secondary languages than older generations, and they are learning more types of languages. Not surprisingly, the English language is dramatically increasing in popularity.
